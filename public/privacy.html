Privacy Policy

YouTube Subscriptions Manager
← Back to app Last updated: 2025-12-13
This is a client-side, static web app. There is no backend server operated by the developer.

What data we process

  • Google sign-in (OAuth): the app requests a Google OAuth access token to call YouTube Data API from your browser.
  • YouTube data: your subscription list (channel IDs/titles) is fetched from YouTube Data API and shown in the UI.
  • Local settings: your language preference may be saved in your browser’s local storage.
  • Files you choose: import files you upload are read and processed in your browser only.

What we collect and store

  • No server-side collection: we do not collect, receive, or store your data on our servers (because there is no server).
  • No selling: we do not sell personal information.

Where your data goes

  • Google: authentication and API access are provided by Google. Requests are sent from your browser to Google endpoints.
  • No other third parties: the app does not intentionally send your data to any other external service.

Security

  • Access tokens are used to call Google APIs from your browser. Treat your browser session as sensitive.
  • When using “unsubscribe/subscribe” features, changes apply immediately to your real YouTube account.

Contact

For questions or requests, contact the developer via: buymeacoffee.com/e3pbwto
